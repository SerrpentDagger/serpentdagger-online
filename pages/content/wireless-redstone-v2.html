Wireless Redstone 1.18

This datapack for Minecraft 1.18 lets you set up wireless redstone transmission by placing ender eyes in item frames on redstone lamps.

The pack allows players in both creative and survival to link these eyes to a numbered channel by using /trigger. You can also use /trigger to check the channel of an eye, or highlight eyes of a given channel.

When a lamp turns on, all other lamps with matching eyes will become redstone blocks until the first turns off again.
